Crosswords lets you generate sparse crossword puzzles out of a given set of words. Sparse means that the puzzle will not be arranged to completely fill a rectangular region (this would correspond to a dense crossword puzzle).

To start out, use the button to set up a list of words and associated clues. Once you're done, press in order to start the crossword generator. Your puzzle will appear within seconds provided that the generator succeeds. Note that the puzzle generation may fail if the specified words don't allow for a proper connection. For instance you cannot build a crossword out of the words 'milk', 'sugar' and 'honey' since they don't have any letters in common. In such a case the application will notify you and suggest that you adapt your list of words.
