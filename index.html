About

CrossWords lets you generate sparse crossword puzzles out of a given set of words. Sparse means that the puzzle will not be arranged to completely fill a rectangular region (this would correspond to a dense crossword puzzle).

To start out, use the button to set up a list of words and associated clues. When you're done hit to start the crossword generator. Your puzzle will appear within seconds provided that the generator succeeds. Note that puzzle generation may fail if the specified words don't allow for a proper connection. For instance your cannot build a crossword out of 'milk', 'sugar' and 'honey' because these words don't have any letters in common. In such a case the application will notify you and suggest that you change your list of words.

The puzzle generator works through your list of words from top to bottom and tries to connect them. If you're not satisfied with the way your puzzle looks you can change the order of your input words with the and buttons and afterwards restart the puzzle generator with . This will possibly but not necessarily yield an alternate puzzle variant.

Individual word clue pairs can be removed with the button. To clear all of your input data as well as any generated puzzle hit .

Finally, you can print teacher and student variants of your puzzle with the buttons. Note that you can choose Print to File in the printing dialog in order to save your puzzle in PDF or PostScript format.
